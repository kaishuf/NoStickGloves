The Problem

During this pandemic, it is necessary for essential workers to wear protective gear while doing their jobs. This includes gloves however for people that deal with stickers such as postage workers or medical workers, latex gloves get in the way. Stickers stick to latex gloves a lot and oftentimes rip the glove. This poses health concerns to such people as well as slow down their productivity because they must worry about their gloves.

Our Purpose

We want to help essential workers dealing with sticky adhesives to continue working with high efficiency while maintaing health precautions.

Our Solution

We have created a cost efficient and reusable glove that will help users to deal with stickers without damaging the glove or the adhesive. Learn more about our product
