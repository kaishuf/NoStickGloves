DIY

Simple 8 step tutorial to make one for yourself

Step 1:

Get an umbrella. The umbrella we used was found at target for $10, however, you can use any umbrella new or used as long as it uses a nylon fabric.



Step 2:

Remove the frame from the fabric. Be careful to not rip or create holes in the fabric. Put aside the frame as it will not be needed to create the glove.



Step 3:

Cut the fabric along the seams into separate parts. You should have some triangles.



Step 4:

Iron the glove on the nylon setting and press it flat. This is an optional step however it would make the process of creating the glove a lot easier.



Step 5:

Take a piece of paper and trace your hand onto the paper. This does not have to be perfect. After the hand has been traced, draw a 1 cm border around the tracing to make the hand bigger.



Step 6:

Cut out the paper hand. Then, stack two of the triangles on top of each other and trace the paper hand onto the triangles. Pin the fabrics together.



Step 7:

Sew the gloves along the lines.



Step 8:

Cut out the sewn glove and fold it inside out. You have now successfully created a No Stick Glove!
